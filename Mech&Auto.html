MECHANICAL AND AUTOMOTIVE

About Mechanical and Automotive

  • Mechanical Trade
    • Training Areas:

    This includes training in areas such as machining, welding, fitting, and maintenance of mechanical equipment. Students learn to work with different types of machinery and tools, understand mechanical drawings, and gain skills in assembling and disassembling mechanical components.

    Career Opportunities:

    Graduates can work as machinists, maintenance technicians, mechanical fitters, CNC operators, and in various roles within manufacturing industries.
